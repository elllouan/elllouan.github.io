Work philosophy

First off, I would like to introduce my goals. As I am entering my fourth year in my engineering studies, I realised that I needed to bring more tangibility into my work by documenting it. My mindset could be summarized in these words:

  • Curiosity: Especially for the fields of electronics and computer engineering.
  • Proactivity: Being inquisitive and asking for feedback.
  • Resourceful: Being able to self-teach or make effective researchs on subjects or topics when necessary.
  • Persistence: Trying as much as possible to build a deep and long-term knowledge.
  • Reflective: Leveraging my past experiences to serve better my current purpose.

I wish to steer my studies more in the field of embedded systems. I am really interested in signal processing (my major this year), control theory, low-level programmation and electronics. In parallel to my engineering studies, I will also be studying management (MAE) for the next 2 years at the Brest IAE as part of a double-diploma master cursus. By doing so, I yearn to expand my skills beyond my technical and scientific knowledge. Nowadays, I think that an enginner should also be considering social skills as they are essential in enhancing team work, well-being, performance and productivity.
