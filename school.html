Signal Processing

Our purpose in this course is to understand what are the impacts of the sampling on an analog signal.

Keywords: Sound/Image processing, Laplace/Fourier, Filters, Analog/Discrete signals

1. Discrete Fourier Transform (TFD) and Analog Filter Synthesis

First off, let's remind us of the TFD.

  1. 1. Sampling & Quantification:
    turns the continuous signal into a discrete signal.
  2. 2. Temporal truncation of the signal:
    get an finite amount of samplings.
  3. 3. Spectral discretization:
    turns the continuous spectrum into a discrete spectrum.
  4. 4. Zero-Padding (optional):
    increase the spectrum's resolution.
